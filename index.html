Fruit Cake Recipe

Fruit cake recipe will help you make soft and moist fruit cake in an easy method. This is one of the simplest and easiest version of making a traditional fruit cake. In this fruit cake recipe, we boil the dried fruits in liquid which will make the raisins and dried fruits soft and plumpy and thus the cake turns out deliciously moist. The method of making this super soft fruit cake is one of the least messy ways that we can a fruit cake.
